The Unsolved Problem

A Growing Sustainability Gap
in Vietnam’s Agriculture

Chemical fertiliser overuse is degrading Vietnam’s soil and water, while 7.3 million tons of fruit waste go unmanaged each year. With only 30–50% of their nutrients are absorbed by plants, the rest is lost through runoff, evaporation, and seepage. This leads to serious water, air, and soil pollution
